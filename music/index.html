Music

Music has always been a part of my life, and since 2008 I have had a personal music project on an on-and-off basis. I took a break from creating music in order to focus more on my programming skills, but now I have gotten back into music production again.

I tend to produce semi-experimental electronic music spanning different genres. My DAW of choice is Renoise, an excellent tracker. In addition to this I use various plugins and samples. I have also had my way with Cubase and Ableton Live, though I now only use Renoise.

You will find my complete discography at Bandcamp, while you can find some songs and more random clips at SoundCloud.
